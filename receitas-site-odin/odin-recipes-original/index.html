Brazil Desserts

Being borned and raised in the country I have to say...brazilians love sweets!

Indeed brazil is a country full of flavors and many original dishes to try! The desserts play a big role in the country. What would be a B-day party without "Brigadeiro"? Condesend Milk flan is a go to for any ocasion! Quindim originally from north of brazil makes succes in every state!
