Brigadeiro

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Codesend milk
  • 1 Table spoon of butter
  • 3 table spoons of cocoa
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Directions

The most easy and decilious desserts ever!

  1. In a pan (low heat) just add all the ingredients together
  2. Mix everything untill it starts getting thick
  3. Wait untill it coll down and make litle balls with the mix
  4. Add sprinkls over the brigadeiros balls and leave in the fridge for at least 2 hours

    5. Enjoy it!
