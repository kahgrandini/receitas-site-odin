Condesend Milk Flan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1 can sweetened condesend milk
  • 1 cup of milk (you can use the consedend milk can as measure)

Directions:

Simple and easy!

  1. First preheat the oven in 350F
  2. Mix the milk, Eggs and the Condesend milk in the blender for about 3 minuts
  3. After mixing, put the sugar in a pan and wait untill it melt completely
  4. Add the melted sugar into the baking dish and add the blender mix afeter it
  5. Now you going to need a larger bowl or pain the can fits the baking dish
  6. Add water in the bowl and put the baking dish iver it (water bath)
  7. Put it in the oven and Wait for about 30 minuts
  8. Unmold it and put it on the fridge for at least 2 hours

Enjoy the delicious dessert!
